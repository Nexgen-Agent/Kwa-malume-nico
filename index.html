About Malume Nico

Discover the story behind the best kota in Tzaneen

Our Story

Malume Nico started as a small street food stall with a big dream - to serve the most delicious, authentic kota in Mavele Village. What began as a passion project has now become a local institution.

Our secret? We use only the freshest ingredients, traditional recipes, and a whole lot of love in every kota we make. From our classic Russian kota to our specialty creations, each bite tells a story.

Whether you're grabbing a quick bite on your lunch break or hanging out with friends on the weekend, Malume Nico is the place to be for great food and good vibes.
